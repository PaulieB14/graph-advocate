Look for 12+ month track records, healthy redemption pressure (< 0.30), diversified depositors, and clean liquidation history. Realistic outcomes for a good vault: +5–60% / year, -30 to -50% in a bad year. This page is research — it does not execute trades, custody funds, or give financial advice.
